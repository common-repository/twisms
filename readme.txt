Use Twilio to send SMS (text messages) from your website!

== Description ==
Text messaging is one of the best ways to get your customers attention. The TwiSMS plugin makes it easy to add that ability to your own website. Using your Twilio account\'s SID, auth token, and phone number you can directly message your customers from within your website.

Please note that this plugin is neither affiliated with nor endorsed by Twilio.

Checkout our other plugins at [plainplugins.altervista.org](https://plainplugins.altervista.org) or contact us to build your own custom plugin!

== Installation ==
1. Create a Twilio account if you do not have one already. You can create one here: [Sign Up for your Twilio account] (https://www.twilio.com/referral/jglM1h)
1. Find your Twilio SID and Auth Token
1. Find your Twilio phone number. You will need to acquire an SMS capable phone number through Twilio if you don\'t have one yet.
1. Enter your Twilio SID, Auth Token, and Phone Number in the TwiSMS Settings page

That\'s it! You should now be able to send text messages from your website!
